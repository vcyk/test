<r><H1><s># </s>PiCARE QUICK START REFERENCE GUIDE</H1>


<H2><s>## </s><EM><s>_</s><STRONG><s>**</s>PICARE is a uniform and streamlined bugs and issues tracking platform<e>**</e></STRONG><e>_</e></EM>.<e> </e></H2>
<p><STRONG><s>**</s>All Pi Network pioneers can post reports about bugs or issues for each and every Pi App running on Pi Browser. Others can share similar experiences under the same topic. Core Team, moderators and Pi Apps developers can gather more info and report that the bug or issue is resolved.<e>**</e></STRONG></p>

<p><STRONG><s>**</s>On Pi Browser, once authenticated you are automatically registered and signed in using your Pi username.<e>**</e></STRONG></p>

<H4><s>#### </s><EM><s>_</s><STRONG><s>**</s>Using this guide, you can quickly test out to see how a bug or issue can be reported, tracked and resolved for any Pi app.<e>**</e></STRONG><e>_</e></EM></H4>


<H2><s>## </s>Role play (testing out to see how a bug or issue can be reported, tracked and resolved for any Pi app):</H2>

<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p><EM><s>_</s><STRONG><s>**</s>The default role for Pi user: nicolas, swarooppoudel, and CryptoGing is Core Team<e>**</e></STRONG><e>_</e></EM></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s><STRONG><s>**</s>Core Team role can assign Core Team, Mod, or any Pi app roles to anyone.<e>**</e></STRONG><e>_</e></EM></p>


<p>By default a newly joined person is automatically assigned Member role. With this role you can immediately report about a bug or issue of a particular Pi app.</p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>For testing purpose, person (A) can self assign a Pi app role for instance shop.pi role.<e>_</e></EM></p>
<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>see section <STRONG><s>**</s>To change your role<e>**</e></STRONG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Person (B) may report about a shop.pi app Bug.<e>_</e></EM></p>
<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>see section <STRONG><s>**</s>To report about a bug or issue of a Pi app<e>**</e></STRONG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Person (A) having the shop.pi role will receive push notification of the Bug reported by person (B).<e>_</e></EM></p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>More people can join in the same discussion to report similar bug or issue.<e>_</e></EM></p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Person (A) may gather more info from person (B).<e>_</e></EM></p>
<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>see section <STRONG><s>**</s>To activate private discussion to gather more info about a bug or issue<e>**</e></STRONG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>When the bug or issue is resolved, person (A) can mark it resolved.<e>_</e></EM></p>
<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>see section <STRONG><s>**</s>To mark a reported bug or issue as resolved<e>**</e></STRONG></p></QUOTE>



<H2><s>## </s>Push notifications:</H2>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Core Team role will receive <STRONG><s>**</s>push notifications<e>**</e></STRONG> of any reports about bugs/issues/suggestions of a Pi Network app<e>_</e></EM> currently including chat.pi, wallet.pi, brainstorm.pi, mine.pi, develop.pi, and Node.</p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Pi app role will receive <STRONG><s>**</s>push notifications<e>**</e></STRONG> of any reports about bugs/issues/suggestions of the same Pi app,<e>_</e></EM> for instance shop.pi role will be notified of reports for shop.pi only.</p>



<H2><s>## </s>To change your role:</H2>

<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>Tap the menu icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-menu.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-menu.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap your Pi username<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Settings<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap options icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Edit<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Check the role group<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Save Changes<e>**</e></STRONG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Some groups<FP char="’">'</FP> checkboxes are disabled. You cannot assign those roles.<e>_</e></EM></p>


<H2><s>## </s>To change another person<FP char="’">'</FP>s role:</H2>

<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>Tap on the person<FP char="’">'</FP>s avatar. If the person<FP char="’">'</FP>s avatar cannot be found on your screen, ask the person send you a private discussion to request for the role.<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap options icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Edit<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Check the role group<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Save Changes<e>**</e></STRONG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>Some groups<FP char="’">'</FP> checkboxes are disabled. You cannot assign those roles.<e>_</e></EM></p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>If yourself is just a Member role, you cannot assign any role. Change your own role first.<e>_</e></EM><br/>
<EM><s>_</s><STRONG><s>**</s>Member can change own role only in this testing. This will be revoked prior to going live.<e>**</e></STRONG><e>_</e></EM></p>


<H2><s>## </s>To report about a bug or issue of a Pi app:</H2>

<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>On home screen, tap Draft icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-draft.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-draft.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Choose Tags<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Choose the app for reporting the bug or issue<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Choose type of reporting - bug, issue, or suggestion<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap Tick icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-tick.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-tick.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Enter title and content<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap Save icon to save as draft  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-save.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-save.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>X<e>**</e></STRONG> icon to exit<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap Send icon to finish and send out  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-send.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-send.jpg)</e></IMG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>If you accidentally exit, the content is not lost. You can always resume by tapping <FP char="“">"</FP>Start a Discussion<FP char="”">"</FP> at bottom of screen, provided you have not closed the PICARE app.<e>_</e></EM></p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>If you have saved draft, you can always resume by tapping <STRONG><s>**</s>Drafts<e>**</e></STRONG> from the menu, even if you have previously closed the PICARE app.<e>_</e></EM></p>



<H2><s>## </s>To activate private discussion to gather more info about a bug or issue:</H2>

<p><EM><s>_</s>A private discussion is visible only to the recipients of the discussion, ie. between the Pi app support and the person who reported the bug or issue.<e>_</e></EM></p>

<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>Tap on the person<FP char="’">'</FP>s avatar.<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap the options icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Send the person a message<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Enter private discussion title and content<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap Save icon to save as draft  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-save.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-save.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>X<e>**</e></STRONG> icon to exit<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap Send icon to finish and send out  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-send.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-send.jpg)</e></IMG></p></QUOTE>

<p><EM><s>_</s>If you accidentally exit, the content is not lost. You can always resume by tapping <FP char="“">"</FP>Start a Discussion<FP char="”">"</FP> at bottom of screen, provided you have not closed the PICARE app.<e>_</e></EM></p>

<p><EM><s>_</s>If you have saved draft, you can always resume by tapping <STRONG><s>**</s>Drafts<e>**</e></STRONG> from the menu, even if you have previously closed the PICARE app.<e>_</e></EM></p>



<H2><s>## </s>To mark a reported bug or issue as resolved:</H2>

<p><EM><s>_</s>You must have Pi app role to mark as resolved for a bug or issue related to the same Pi app only.<e>_</e></EM><br/>
<EM><s>_</s>Core Team can mark as resolved for a Pi Network app<e>_</e></EM> currently including chat.pi, wallet.pi, brainstorm.pi, mine.pi, develop.pi, and Node.</p>

<QUOTE><i>&gt;</i><p>Go to the discussion containing the reported bug or issue<br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap the options icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-options.jpg)</e></IMG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Edit Tags<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap <STRONG><s>**</s>Solved<e>**</e></STRONG><br/>
<i>&gt;</i>Tap Tick icon  <IMG alt="" src="https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-tick.jpg"><s>![</s><e>](https://picare.cf/PIFORUM/public/assets/files/icons/icon-tick.jpg)</e></IMG></p></QUOTE>
